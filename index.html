Learn more here

Thunder Bay is located on Lake Superior in northwestern Ontario, Canada. Known for its many attractions, such as hiking, fishing, and scenic views like the Sleeping Giant (seen above)! Come check us out along with many sights to see outside of our city like Kakabeka Falls!

With the beautiful sights to see, the Marina is one of the most popular spots in Thunder Bay. You can check out the splash pad in the summer and the ice rink in the winter. Take a walk around the trails to check out the boats and the popular Sleeping Giant. While checking out the Marina, dont't forget to stop and read the signs to learn some facts about Thunder Bay! Keep an eye out for the Canadian Geese, they may look friendly but they're pretty aggressive.
